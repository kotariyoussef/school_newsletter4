About LBC

Student voices shaping tomorrow's journalism

Our Mission

LBC is a student-run publication dedicated to providing a platform for young journalists to develop their skills, share diverse perspectives, and report on stories that matter to our community.

Founded in {{ 2025 }}, we believe in the power of student voices and are committed to journalistic excellence, integrity, and innovation.

Our Values

Truth

We are committed to factual reporting and thorough research, ensuring our readers receive accurate and reliable information.

Diversity

We celebrate diverse perspectives and strive to represent all voices within our community and beyond.

Growth

We provide a supportive environment for students to develop their journalistic and creative writing skills.

Our Story

LBC was established in 2025 by a group of passionate students who wanted to create a platform where young writers could share their voices and develop professional journalistic skills.

Since then, we have grown from a small club into a recognized publication with a dedicated team of student writers, editors, photographers, and designers. Our alumni have gone on to work at major news outlets and publications around the world.

Today, we continue to uphold our founding principles while embracing new technologies and storytelling formats to engage with our readers.
